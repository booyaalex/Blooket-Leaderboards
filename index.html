What is This?

Blooket Leaderboards is a website that showcases leaderboards ranking the achievements of many different blooket players. You can see leaderboards from a wide variety of different cattegories, from gamemodes, to stats, even to the leaderboards from blooket events. This website is here to showcase the skill of any and all blooket players.
